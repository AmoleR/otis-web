Advice for writing hints

Hints should get solvers un-stuck. If someone reads all the hints on ARCH and still cannot finish, then the hints didn't do their job. So, try to be pretty generous, rather than subtle.

In particular, prefer explicit instructions over vague pointers: for example, instead of writing just "use generating functions", prefer "construct a generating function $F(X)$ such that the coefficients of $F(X)^2$ match the left-hand sum".
